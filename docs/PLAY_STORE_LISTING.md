```
Velunee is your personal AI companion — always ready to help you think, decide,
and move forward with confidence.

ASK ANYTHING
Chat naturally by text and get clear, thoughtful answers. Ask for study help,
everyday decisions, planning, writing, or a second opinion. Velunee turns a
quick one-liner into a genuinely useful response.

MADE PERSONAL
Choose how Velunee talks to you — warm, concise, playful, or professional — and
set how long answers should be. Velunee remembers helpful details (when you let
it) so conversations feel continuous, not repetitive.

YOUR CONVERSATIONS, SAVED SECURELY
Pick up where you left off. Your chats are stored with strong encryption, and
you're always in control of your data.

A KIND COMMUNITY
Share a thought, ask a question, and support others with hearts and comments in
a lightweight, positive community feed.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
- Your messages are encrypted at rest.
- We never put AI provider secrets in the app.
- Delete your account and all your data anytime, right from Settings.

Sign in with email, or start instantly as a guest.

Ask. Decide. Shine.
```
