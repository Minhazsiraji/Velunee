# Delete your Velunee account

Velunee lets you permanently delete your account and personal data at any
time. Google Play requires this page to be reachable at a public URL; host it
(for example at `https://velunee.app/delete-account`) and reference it in the
Play Console under **App content → Data safety → Data deletion**.

## Delete from inside the app (recommended)

1. Open Velunee and sign in.
2. Go to the **Profile** tab.
3. Tap **Settings**.
4. Under **Account**, tap **Delete account**.
5. Type `DELETE` to confirm, then tap **Delete Forever**.

Your account is deleted immediately and you are signed out.

## What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes:

- Your profile and display name
- Your app preferences
- All conversations and messages
- Saved personal memories
- Community posts, comments, and reactions
- Your authentication record (email/login)

This action **cannot be undone**.

## What may be retained, and for how long

- Encrypted backups are purged on our rolling cycle within **30 days**.
- Anonymized, aggregated statistics that cannot identify you may be retained.
- Records we must keep for legal or fraud-prevention reasons are retained only
  as long as legally required.

## Can't access the app?

Email **privacy@velunee.app** from the address associated with your account
and request deletion. We will verify ownership and delete your data within 30
days.
